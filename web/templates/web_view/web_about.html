About Us


Based on values of good health, environment conservation, nourishment, and care, Vriksh brings to you all a range of pure, natural, and certified organic food products procured from the regions alongside Himalayan ranges. Vriksh works with a large number of accredited farmers who do not use or promote the use of pesticides, chemical fertilizers and genetically modified seeds to grow their produce.

For the perfect nutrition and healthy life, Vriksh aims to provide its customers healthy chemical-free foods and other products. Organic food products, brought to you by Vriksh, are grown under a system of agriculture which is environmentally and socially responsible.

From snacks and breakfast to lunch and dinner, Vriksh is a one stop shop for all your nutritional needs and healthy life. With a vast network of suppliers and delivery partners, let yourself rest and relax while we deliver your packet of health to your doorstep!
